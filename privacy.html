Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 September 2025

DearAfter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to respecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

1. What personal data we collect

When you download our product or subscribe to our mailing list, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Payment information (via Gumroad – not stored by us)
  • Any messages you send to our support team

2. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Deliver purchased products
  • Send transactional and optional marketing emails
  • Provide support and respond to enquiries
  • Improve the product experience

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Cookies

We may use basic cookies or analytics tools (e.g. Plausible, Simple Analytics) to understand site usage. No tracking cookies are used unless consented.

4. Payment processing

All payments are securely handled by Gumroad.com. We do not store card or payment details on our servers.

5. Data retention

We keep customer data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined above, or as required by UK law.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to or correction of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise any rights, contact us at: support@dearafter.co.uk

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email: support@dearafter.co.uk
