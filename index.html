Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I start?

Use the Quick-Start Checklist to begin in ~30 minutes with 1–2 prompts. Most people finish their first letter in one sitting.

What exactly is included?

Main Guide (16p), Goodbye Letter Prompts (12p), Memory Capsule Worksheets (8p), Executor Guide (6p), Cultural Prompts (4p), Grandkids Letter (4p), Family Dynamics Assessment (2p), How-To-Use (4p), Quick-Start (1p). Total: 57 pages.

I'm 73 and not comfortable with computers. Is this too technical?

Not at all. Everything comes as printable documents. You can fill them out on your computer OR print them and write by hand - whatever feels more comfortable. Many customers prefer the handwritten approach as it feels more personal.

Will my family think this is morbid or depressing?

Quite the opposite. Most families tell us they wish they'd done this sooner because it opens beautiful conversations and brings families closer. This is about love and connection, not death and sadness.

I already have a will - do I need this?

Your legal will is essential for asset protection. DearAfter adds the emotional layer that legal documents can't provide – helping your family understand not just what you decided, but why you love them.

Is this legal advice?

No. This is an emotional companion to your legal will and works with any solicitor or will service. This emotional companion kit provides organization templates and memory-keeping tools only. These materials are designed to work alongside your professionally-prepared legal will. For all legal matters relating to your will, estate planning, or inheritance, please consult with a qualified solicitor or legal professional.
